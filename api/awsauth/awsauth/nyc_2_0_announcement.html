Hello CovidActNow API users,

We are reaching out to let you know about an upcoming change to how data for New York City is represented in our API.

Historically, weâ€™ve represented data for NYC in such a way that all data from the 5 counties of New York City were aggregated into the New York County (fips: 36061). Queries for the other 4 counties (Kings, Queens, Bronx, and Richmond) would return a 404.

Going forward, we are removing the aggregation of all NYC county data into one county - API data will exist for each of the 5 NYC counties.

At the same time, we have recently added support for CBSAs to our API.

CBSAs represent collections of counties that are socioeconomically linked. They are used to represent metropolitan and micropolitan areas.

For those interested in the aggregate data for the New York Metro Area, it is available here:

https://api.covidactnow.org/v2/cbsa/35620.json?apiKey=YOUR_API_KEY

(Note that the metro area is broader than the bounds of New York City).

We are planning on making the change to the API mid next week but please let us know if this is going to impact your use of the API adversely and we can adjust that timing.
